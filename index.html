#DidYouKnow🇨🇬

Capoeira originated in the Democratic Republic of the Congo!

#DidYouKnow 🇧🇷

Capoeira was established in Brazil in the 16th as Portuguese settlers brought slaves from Africa to work on sugar cane plantations.

#DidYouKnow 🥷🏼

In the 19th century, Capoeiristas were used as bodyguards or even hitmen. Later in the century, it was made illigal as the Brazilian government was afraid of criminals training their fighting abilities.

#DidYouKnow 🤸🏽‍♀️🌎

Capoeira started being spread worldwide during the 1970s. In 2014 Capoeira was granted special protected heritage status by UNESCO
